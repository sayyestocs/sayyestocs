About Say Yes To CS



We are Say Yes To CS! We believe that learning computer science should be accessible to everyone. After participating in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, our team decided to create Say Yes To CS to make learning computer science fun, easy, and approachable. Through interactive tutorials, we help people understand how to utilize code to create personalized websites and what's going on in the tech world. Together, we can all Say Yes To CS!
