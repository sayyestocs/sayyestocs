At Say Yes To CS we want to inspire and interest people in the world of computer science. Our curriculum is laid out from top to bottom. To begin, you must download some software to allow you to run your code. HTML is the base of your website. CSS is the styling and formatting. At first your website may not look pretty, but as you follow the tutorials and start on the CSS it will get more complex and more interesting looking! So be patient and don't get frustrated because the end product will look great! If you ever need help email us!
