Learn HTML Interactive

In the space below, you can play around with various HTML <tags>. In HTML, you must always <open> and </close> tags. Some of the tags you may explore:
  • <h1>, <h2>, <h3>: headers of sizes 1 (largest) through 3 (smallest)
  • <p>: paragraph (can be 1+ lines)

  • <b>,<i>, <sup>, <sub>: bold, italics, superscript, and subscript
  • href: link a website
  • <img>: add an image
  • <ul>: an unordered list
  • <ol>: an ordered list
