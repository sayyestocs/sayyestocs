Learn CSS Interactive

In the space below, you can play around with various CSS features. In CSS, we use curly brackets, {}, to open and close declarations, which specify what HTML properties you are altering. Some of the selectors you may explore:
  • Background-color:determines the color of your website's background
  • Font-family:determines the font style of your text
  • Font-size:determines the size of your text
  • Font-color:determines the color of your text
  • Text-alignment:determines where the text is positioned on the page
